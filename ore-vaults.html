Ore Vaults Website & Branding

Branding   |   Information Architecture   |   Wireframes   |   Design  |   Prototype

Overview

As a new business venture, Ore Vaults wanted to establish themselves in the consumer market as a company offering beautiful and secure mail vaults with exceptional customer service for their upscale customers. While Ore Vaults main goal for this website was to provide a convenient online shopping center for home owners, they realize that there is a lot of opportunity with B2B retailers and contractors. With this in mind, I was able to create a website that is largely focused on the end consumer, but has clear links and information geared towards these retailers. Through rich colors and elegant typography, I was able to establish a high-end brand experience while handling the product photography challenges that were associated with the project.
