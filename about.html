In the beginning

Hailing from the suburbs of Cincinnati, I've come a long way from the little corner of Ohio that's not quite sure if it's Southern, Northern, or Mid-western (at least, I'm not sure). After studying Visual Communication Technology at Bowling Green State University, where I effectively found my passion for design, I moved on down I-75 to join Pyxl's talented team as a graphic designer in Knoxville, Tennessee.

2012

While living in Tennessee, I gained experience in the many realms of design and agency life, including: web design, digital campaign marketing, information architecture, persona analysis, motion graphics, branding, infographics, brochures, event collateral, posters and even Powerpoint presentations.

2014

In early 2014, I helped blaze a new trail west to establish Pyxl's third office in Boulder. It was here that I narrowed my interests to creating digital applications and I am now dedicating my time to shifting my career in that direction.

Let's be real though, the world doesn't revolve solely around pixels and I fully intend to fill my life with experiences of travel, camping and great people (and pets).
