Hubble Website Design

Information Architecture   |   Wireframes   |   Design   |   Prototype

Overview

Hubble was looking for a new website to represent their recent name, brand and marketing strategy update. As a company, they are all about providing high-performance business solutions to their high-performance audience that struggles with collecting all of their business data in one easily accessible location that has up-to-date information. With this in mind, I took on the challenge of creating a web presence that spoke to both the c-suite and their influencers. A large piece of the site is geared towards educating the users of a relatively new product offering about what problems Hubble can solve for them. The visual aesthetics provide a sense of a high-quality product while also injecting Hubble's quirky personality through bright colors, illustrations and team photos.
